About Mr&Mrs

Welcome to Mr&Mrs Wedding Planners, where we transform your wedding dreams into reality. With over a decade of experience in the wedding industry, we've helped hundreds of couples create the perfect celebration of their love.

At Mr&Mrs, we believe that every wedding should be as unique as the couple themselves. Our personalized approach ensures that your special day reflects your personality, style, and love story. From intimate gatherings to grand celebrations, we handle every detail with precision and care.

Our team of passionate wedding planners combines creativity, organization, and industry expertise to deliver flawless events that exceed expectations. We work closely with a curated network of vendors to bring your vision to life while managing all the logistics behind the scenes.

Whether you're planning a local celebration or a destination wedding, Mr&Mrs is committed to making your journey to "I do" as stress-free and enjoyable as possible.

Our Journey

From a small passion project to a leading wedding planning company

2010

Where It All Began

Mr&Mrs was founded by Emma and James Thompson after planning their own wedding and discovering their passion for creating magical celebrations.

2013

Growing Our Team

As demand for our services grew, we expanded our team with talented planners who share our vision and dedication to excellence.

2016

Award-Winning Service

Mr&Mrs received our first industry recognition, winning "Best Wedding Planner" at the Regional Wedding Awards.

2019

Expanding Horizons

We began offering destination wedding services, helping couples create unforgettable celebrations around the world.

2023

Today

With over 500 weddings in our portfolio, Mr&Mrs continues to grow while maintaining our commitment to personalized service and exceptional experiences.
